About Restoration Church

Our mission is to bring people to the understanding of their new life in Christ. A life of victory, peace, and power. Foundational scripture is 2cor.5v17 that explain unto us the important of our new life. The purpose is to bring people in him so that he can life through them in the earth. We want to bring the nature of Christ to the world. Christ in us the hope of glory. He is the only hope.
